My (Current) Top Ten Songs!

Welcome to my top ten songs page! Here are my top ten songs right now and why!:

  1. Still Got Time - Dexter in the Newsagent

    As mentioned in my About page, this song is one of my favorites, and I listen to it nearly every day at this point. It's just a really good song, and I love the beat.

  2. Agora Hills - Doja Cat

    Say what you will about Doja Cat and her recent PR issues, but this song is a banger. I love the beat, and the lyrics are pretty good too if not a little strange at times.

  3. Souvenir - Bump Of Chicken

    One of the Spy x Family op's and it is still constantly stuck in my head from when I last watched the show. The beat is great and the vibes that go along with it are even better.

  4. I Wish You Cared - Xander

    Maybe a seemlingly strange addition to this list, but this has been my "study song" for as long as I can remember. Good lofi beat and the songs that are usually auto played after are just as good. A very good song if you need to sit down and do some work.

  5. Cherry Wine - Grentperez

    Just an all time classic. I've been listening to this song for years now and it never gets old. The beat is great and the lyrics are even better. A very good song to listen to if you're feeling down.

  6. Pool - Still Woozy, Remi Wolf

    Also one of my favorites for a while. Excelelnt beat and and lyrics. Very chill Song that I could listen to over and over again. Also Remi Wolf is ofc amazing.

  7. Sexy Villian - Remi Wolf

    Another Remi Wolf favorite. Just a great beat with easy to sing lyrics. I love the vibes that this song gives off and it's just a great song to listen to whenever.

  8. Roomates - Malcolm Todd

    Found this one on Tik Tok a while back but still listen to it all the time. Great beat and lyrics. I love the chorus and the rest of the song is just as good.

  9. Breakup Season - Maya Delilah, Samm Henshaw

    A medium paced love song but I love the dynamic between the two singers. Just very pleasing to listen too - feels good on the ears. I love the chorus the most but once again the rest of the song is very good.

  10. Falling Behind - Laufey

    Honestly, should have been more Laufey songs on this list but they remind me of sad jujustu kaisen Tik Tok edits so I didn't want to add too many. This song is just a good listen whether you're trying to fall asleep, workout, or do anything at all.
